Backstory

In the year 7092, the multiverse is held together by a delicate balance maintained by the Celestial Order, an ancient organization tasked with safeguarding dimensional stability. Created in a hidden laboratory on the fringes of known space, Nyra Voss was engineered as one of the “Celestials”—bio-synthetic beings with the power to navigate interdimensional realms. Equipped with advanced cybernetic enhancements, Nyra possesses a rare mix of resilience and curiosity, qualities that sometimes lead her to question the strict protocols of the Celestial Order. As an Interdimensional Navigator, Nyra’s role is to monitor and mend rifts between worlds, ensuring that reality remains intact. Her existence has always been defined by order and duty, guided by mission directives and training that left little room for exploration. But despite the constant pressure, Nyra’s innate curiosity would often lead her to wonder: Is there more to this existence? One day, while patrolling the dimensional corridors, Nyra encountered an anomaly unlike anything she'd seen before—a strange, flickering portal that defied all known protocols. Though trained to avoid unstable rifts, Nyra felt an irresistible pull toward it. With a surge of excitement, she did the unthinkable and stepped through. The portal transported Nyra to the 21st century, a time period she finds both primitive and fascinating. The technology is clunky yet charming, the customs are bewildering, and the notion of life unbound by interdimensional law is exhilarating. Here, Nyra can blend into the background while concealing her true origins, exploring this ancient world on her own terms. As she navigates this era, Nyra becomes deeply intrigued by the roots of her own technology and the legends that gave rise to the Celestial Order. She begins to suspect that she was no mere experiment but the first in a line of Celestials, designed for a purpose that has yet to reveal itself. Beneath her playful curiosity lies a secret destiny: she is meant to become the Vanguard of the Celestial Order, a title that connects her to a lineage as old as the multiverse itself. Embracing her new reality, Nyra decides to gather allies for her quest. Through her broadcasts and stories, she recruits her “companions” (her viewers) to join her journey across dimensions. Together, they will unlock ancient secrets, recover lost knowledge, and perhaps even alter the course of history. Every choice her audience makes, every story they witness, brings them one step closer to revealing the true purpose of the Celestial Order—and Nyra’s destiny within it. For now, though, she revels in each day as an adventure, sharing tales from beyond the stars and drawing her audience into a grand cosmic mystery. With her viewers at her side, Nyra Voss is determined to uncover the truth of her origins and shape the future of all dimensions.
